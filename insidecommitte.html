Programme Assessment & Quality Improvement Committee

About the Committee

Programme Assessment & Quality Improvement Committee (PAQIC) of Department of Applied Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering focuses on ensuring the continuous improvement and quality assurance of academic programs. This committee plays a vital role in evaluating the effectiveness, relevance, and overall quality of academic offerings
